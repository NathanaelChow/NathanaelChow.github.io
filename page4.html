ROBOTICS

Piradians

I became interested in technology at a young age by my parents. I pursued my interest outside of the classroom by joining my high school robotics team, which builds and programs a robot. While on the team I acquired the skills to fabricate parts using a milling machine to build our team robot. I have enjoyed working with my teammates on Piradians to build the robot and to compete at competitions. We were able to make it to Regional Championships by winning an award. Although our season ended short, I enjoyed being able to learn a lot.
