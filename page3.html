APUSH FINAL

McKinley Assassination

The AP US history final project was a group project, reenactment of the assassination of President McKinley. The assignment was to make a video about anything we learned in class throughout the school year. Filming the project was challenging because we had to do a lot of preparation. We prepared before filming by writing a script, collected props and wardrobe, and choosing a location and time. I was in charge of filming and editting. I particularly enjoyed talking with my group and editting all of our creative ideas into a great video.
